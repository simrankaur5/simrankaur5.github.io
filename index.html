Hello! I'm Simran Kaur

I have an interest in the analysis, design, development and evaluation of interactive applications and technology. The aim is to go beyond mere ‘guidelines for good design’ and to apply the principles of interaction, to identify problems with an interface, to understand how these interactions may be improved and to apply the knowledge necessary to be able to design better interfaces.
